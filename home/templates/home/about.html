To sum it up in one sentence: We just love anime!

What's the best after a stressing day? To sit down, take a pen and start drawing. But what is it worth when you have a folder full of pictures that noone will ever see?

That's why we founded Anime Art. We want to share the art of our talented artist Shippudeve, because we believe every anime fan (and even the ones who never saw any anime) will love seeing these art pieces every day on their wall.

We hope you enjoy as much as we authentic artwork that is unique!

Our Products

The products that we sell are all handmade and authentic. You won't find them anywhere in the world, as our artist draws every piece on her on. She doesn't just copy from animes, but gives her artwork a special touch.

We offer two different types of art: Digital drawings (created digitally) and classic drawings (created with paper and pen). At the moment we have many digital drawings in our stock, but of the classic ones there are more to come.

Our products will be all printed on high quality paper, so it looks wonderful in a nice frame!

As we are a small start up company, we are still limited, so right now you can buy the images in only one size (42,0 cm x 59,4 cm / german DIN A2). We are working hard to be able to offer you more options on the sizes.

The Artist

Her name is Shippudeve and a few years back she fell in love with anime. But only watching anime was not enough for her and so she found her creative side and begin to draw... At first only with a pen on paper and with getting better and better she decided to try drawing digitally. To share her passion and hobby with others, she is now selling her art in this online shop.
